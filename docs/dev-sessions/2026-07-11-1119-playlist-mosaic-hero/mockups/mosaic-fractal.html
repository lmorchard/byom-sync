Fractal subdivision — scaling to longer playlists

Most-featured albums keep full quadrants; the long tail packs into subdivided quadrants (each a 2×2 of sub-covers). One level of subdivision only — sub-quadrants don't subdivide further, to stay legible. Gap style from your pick (B).

5–7 albums
3 whole + 1 subdivided quadrant (shows up to 7)
8–10 albums
2 whole + 2 subdivided (shows up to 10)
11–13 albums
1 whole + 3 subdivided (shows up to 13)

Rule: rank distinct covers by track count; the top-ranked stay whole, the least-featured quadrants subdivide to absorb the tail. ≤4 distinct → plain 2×2. >13 → cap at 3 subdivided (rest omitted).
